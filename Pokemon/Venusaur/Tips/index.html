Tips


Venusaur is a good swap against TOXIC and he removes TOXIC SPIKES.


Venusaur is a good swap against grass pokemon like Breloom.


If you can take a hit, use GROWTH. the next turn you will absorb life with GIGA DRAIN.


When using GROWTH beware of FOUL PLAY pokemon like Umbreon.
