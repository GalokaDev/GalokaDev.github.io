Tips


When you have Blissey on the field the opponent will always swap in a fight type.


If you play Blissey offensive try not to show the moves that do damage.


Try to use FLAMETHROWER when you think your opponent is about to put a Scizor.


If you are looking for a full support pokemon tank maybe you should go for Chansey.
